About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic individuals dedicated to promoting innovation, creativity, and technology on our campus. Our chapter is a part of TinkerHub Foundation, a community of tech enthusiasts who believe in the power of collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Our chapter is led by a team of passionate students who work together to organize events, workshops, and projects that cater to the needs of our campus community. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone can learn, explore, and experiment with technology.

Our activities range from technical workshops and hackathons to community-building events and outreach programs. We collaborate with industry experts and academia to create meaningful learning experiences for our members.

We believe that learning should be fun, and that's why we organize a variety of events and activities that engage our members and spark their curiosity. Our goal is to empower our members with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to pursue their passions and make a positive impact on the world.

Whether you're a student interested in technology, entrepreneurship, or social innovation, or simply looking to connect with like-minded individuals, we welcome you to join our community. We believe that together, we can create a better future through innovation and collaboration.
